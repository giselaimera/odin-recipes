Tacos Recipe

Description

Tacos are a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your taste. From meat lovers to vegetarians, there's a taco for everyone. Here's a quick overview of what to expect:

Tacos Ingredients

These are the key ingredients for classic tacos. Feel free to add your favorite toppings and fillings:

  • Taco shells
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Onion and garlic
  • Taco seasoning
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Sour cream

Steps

Making tacos is straightforward and fun. Here's how to do it:

  1. Cook the meat with taco seasoning.
  2. Prepare the taco shells.
  3. Assemble the tacos with your favorite toppings.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

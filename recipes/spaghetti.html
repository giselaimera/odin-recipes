Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish that is simple to make yet bursting with flavor. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but here's a quick overview:

Spaghetti Ingredients

This spaghetti recipe is versatile and easy to customize. Stick to the original ingredients or modify them as needed. Here's what you'll need:

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground meat
  • Garlic and onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

Follow these steps to make your spaghetti:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Mix the sauce with the spaghetti.
  4. Top with Parmesan cheese and serve.
